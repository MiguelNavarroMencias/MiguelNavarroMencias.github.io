The Greatest and Most Reliable Farm in Honduras
Hacienda Los Angeles, founded by Sr. Miguel Navarro in 2012, is an estate in Juticalpa, Olancho, that partners with local farmers to mechanize land and produce high-quality crops. Dedicated to supporting the agricultural community, the estate offers various services like land mechanization and crop cultivation with transparent pricing, aiming to be the go-to estate for farmers in the region.
>

We have a variety of products available:
Coffee: One of the main exports, with high-quality beans grown in various regions.
Bananas and Plantains: Widely cultivated and exported.
Sugarcane: Used for producing sugar and other byproducts.
Pineapples: Grown for both local consumption and export.
Cacao: Used for producing chocolate and other cocoa products.
Citrus Fruits: Such as oranges and lemons.
Vegetables: Including tomatoes, peppers, and onions.
