About Us

Welcome to Hacienda Los Angeles, an estate owned by the Navarro family, located in the heart of Juticalpa, Olancho. Our journey began in 2012 with our founder, Sr. Miguel Navarro, who envisioned a thriving estate that collaborates with local farmers to mechanize land and cultivate high-quality products.

Our Mission

At Hacienda Los Angeles, we are dedicated to providing a range of services and products to the public. Our mission is to support the agricultural community in Olancho by offering reliable and efficient services that help farmers achieve their goals.

Our Services

We offer a variety of services tailored to meet the needs of commercial and individual farmers. Our services include land mechanization, crop cultivation, and more. Each service is designed to provide detailed information and transparent pricing, ensuring our customers have all the information they need to make informed decisions.

Why Choose Us?

Creating a website for Hacienda Los Angeles allows us to expand our reach and connect with more customers. It provides a direct and accurate source of information about our business, eliminating the need for external sources. Our goal is to secure recurring services from our customers by offering exceptional service and building long-term relationships.

Our Vision

We aim to be the go-to estate for farmers in Olancho, providing top-notch services and products that contribute to the growth and success of the agricultural community. With an estimated 200 visitors per month, we are committed to serving our customers with dedication and excellence.
